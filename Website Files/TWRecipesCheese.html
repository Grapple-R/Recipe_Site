Grilled Cheese

Prep Time: 5 minutes | Total Time: 20 minutes | Servings: 2 Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread or sour dough
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 ounces cheddar cheese or 1 American Cheese Slice

Directions

  1. Preheat a small skillet over low heat.
  2. Spread butter over one side of each piece of bread and place butter side down in the skillet.
  3. Top with cheddar cheese slices, black pepper to taste and remaining slice of bread, butter side out.
  4. Grill until golden, about 4-5 minutes. Flip and grill the other side until golden.
